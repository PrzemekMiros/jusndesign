**JD-12 Truck Bed Slide** is a heavy-duty slide-out platform designed for pickup truck beds of all sizes – small, mid-size, and full-size.  
It provides quick and convenient access to cargo placed deep inside the bed, without the need to climb into the truck bed or unload everything.

### Compatibility
The JD-12 platform is compatible with many pickup models, including:
* JT
* Ford F-150 Raptor
* RAM
* Toyota Tundra
* Ford Ranger
* Toyota Tacoma
* Toyota Hilux
* Shark
* Other pickup models with suitable mounting points

### Available Sizes (mm)
* **Large:** 1230 × 1631
* **Medium:** 1156 × 1441
* **Small:** 1086 × 1486

### Key Features
* **Universal application:** Fits small, mid-size, and full-size pickups.
* **Heavy-duty construction:** Designed to handle heavy loads and demanding working conditions.
* **Smooth sliding mechanism:** Provides fast and easy access to tools, equipment, and cargo.
* **Non-invasive installation:** Bolt-on mounting without drilling or permanent vehicle modifications.
* **Multiple size options:** Three size variants allow the platform to be tailored to different truck bed dimensions.

### Installation
* **Installation type:** Non-invasive (bolt-on)
* **Difficulty level:** 2 / 5
* **Professional installation required:** No

JD-12 is the solution for those who want **real access to their cargo**, not just a good-looking truck bed that’s impossible to reach without acrobatics.
