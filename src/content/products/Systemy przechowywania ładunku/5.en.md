The **JD-12 Truck Bed Slide** is a heavy-duty sliding tray system designed for pickup trucks of all sizes – small, medium, and full-size.  
It significantly improves access to cargo stored deep inside the truck bed while maintaining a non-destructive, bolt-on installation.

### Compatibility
The JD-12 Truck Bed Slide is compatible with a wide range of pickup truck models, including:
* JT
* Ford F-150 Raptor
* RAM
* Toyota Tundra
* Ford Ranger
* Toyota Tacoma
* Toyota Hilux
* Shark
* Other pickup truck models with compatible mounting points

### Available Sizes (mm)
* **Large:** 1230 × 1631
* **Medium:** 1156 × 1441
* **Small:** 1086 × 1486

### Key Features
* **Universal Application:** Suitable for small, medium, and large pickup trucks.
* **Heavy-Duty Construction:** Designed to handle demanding loads and harsh working conditions.
* **Smooth Sliding Mechanism:** Allows easy access to tools, luggage, and equipment stored in the truck bed.
* **Non-destructive Installation:** Bolt-on mounting without drilling or permanent vehicle modifications.
* **Multiple Size Options:** Available in three size variants to fit different truck bed dimensions.

### Installation
* **Mounting Type:** Non-destructive, bolt-on installation
* **Difficulty Level:** 2 / 5
* **Professional Installation Required:** No

The JD-12 Truck Bed Slide is an ideal solution for users who need fast, safe, and convenient access to their pickup truck cargo.
