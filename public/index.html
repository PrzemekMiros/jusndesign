Modification Accessories

Your place for off-road accessories

We specialize in supplying high-quality parts for modifying off-road pickup and truck vehicles. Our products are designed for real-world off-road use as well as everyday driving, which is why they combine increased durability, improved handling, and enhanced comfort.

We focus on continuous innovation

We are passionate about off-roading and outdoor activities, which is why we organize off-road expeditions for our members every month. We consistently analyze the weak points of factory OE parts and, based on that analysis as well as proven solutions from established international brands, we introduce improvements and upgrades.

Quality over cost savings

We operate a full production facility along with a CNC machining center. For key components, we use aluminum alloys and carbon steel, with the most critical parts machined using CNC technology. Surface finishing is carried out through anodizing and sandblasting. Cost savings achieved through an efficient production process are passed directly on to our customers.
